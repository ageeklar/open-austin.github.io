We Are Open Austin

Open government, open data, and civic apps in Austin, TX.
  • {% include icons/capital.svg %} We advocate to make government work for the 21st century.
  • {% include icons/calendar.svg %} We host frequent meetups, hack projects and events. Join us!
  • {% include icons/brigade.svg %} We develop open-source projects as a Code for America Brigade.

Our Events

Every month Open Austin hosts three events:

  • Open Gov & Civic Tech Meetup - We invite a speaker from the civic tech community and try cover a broad range of topics. Normally at the Terrazas public library.
  • Civic Hack Night - We try to invite a non-profit or anybody with an idea for a civic tech project to come pitch their ideas. Then we break out into project-specific groups and work for about two hours. Normally at WeWork on 6th & Congress.
  • Civic Hack Saturday - Like the Civic Hack Night but on Saturday's and without presentations. This is a comfortable space to come and work on a project for a few hours. Usually at Urban Co-Lab.
